Welcome to Our Beautiful Village Beyong

Beyoung, a small village in west Sikkim. Located approximately 3 km from Sombaria West Sikkim. Beyoung is a destination for those seeking a peaceful getaway.

Tourist Attractions

Barsey

The Barsey Rhododendron Sanctuary is a 104 square kilometer protected area in the Singalila Range of West Sikkim. It's known for its rhododendron blooms, diverse wildlife, and trekking opportunities. .

Gorkhey

Gorkhey is a small village situated on the border between West Sikkim and West Bengal, nestled in a valley between Darjeeling and Sikkim.

Allaytar

Allaytar is a small village situated in West Sikkim known for its stunning views of the Himalayas and is a popular spot for trekking and picnic.
